What we are doing




Questionnaire

We want to know more about how people manage their personal health information and how they communicate with their physician.

We are developing an Android app to record consultations, transcribe them, and give definitions for all the medical terms used.


Research

We will test and evaluate the app, from its usability to the quality of its transcriptions..
