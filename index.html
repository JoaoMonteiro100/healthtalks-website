Details

This mobile app was developed with your well-being in mind. HealthTalks helps you manage your personal health information, remember your physician's advice, and understand your health status.

HealthTalks' main features are:

  • Recording the audio of a medical appointment;
  • Transcription of the dialogue to text;
  • In the transcribed text, finding medical concepts and define them;
  • Allowing an easy management and organization of your appointments.

Features

All in one place

All of your appointments, your personal health information, and custom notes; everything in one single app.

Search made easy

You can set your physician's name and specialty for each appointment so that you can easily find it later.

Absolute privacy

All of your private information is stored locally on your device, which means that no one but you can access it.

Efficient and intuitive

HealthTalks' simple and familiar interface was developed for every type of user and technological proficiency level.

Simplified concepts

If you have difficulty understanding your physician's speech, you can now check the definitions of every concept.

For the whole family

Because you can specify who is the patient in each appointment, you can organize your family's appointments.
